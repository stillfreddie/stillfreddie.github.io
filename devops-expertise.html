DevOps Expertise

Cloud Infrastructure Automation

CI/CD Pipeline Engineering

DevSecOps and Governance

IAM least privilege, secrets management, and policy as code. Secure pipelines and compliant deployments.

Cost and Performance Optimization

Right-sizing, autoscaling, and caching. Track usage and reduce spend without sacrificing reliability.
